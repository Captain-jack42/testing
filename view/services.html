Visa Information

Travel Visa

A visa is a conditional authorization granted by a territory to a foreigner, allowing them to enter, remain within, or leave that territory. Visas typically include limits on the duration of the foreigner's stay, areas within the country they may enter, the dates they may enter, the number of permitted visits, or if the individual can work in the country in question.

Types of Visas:

  • Tourist Visa
  • Business Visa
  • Student Visa
  • Work Visa
  • Transit Visa
