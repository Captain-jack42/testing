Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I make a booking?

You can make a booking through our website by:

  • Selecting your desired service (flights, hotels, packages)
  • Choosing your preferences and dates
  • Following the simple booking process
  • Creating an account or logging in to complete the booking

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various secure payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • UPI
  • Popular Digital Wallets

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary depending on the service and provider. Generally, cancellations made 48 hours before the service date are eligible for a full refund. Please check specific terms during booking.

How can I modify my booking?

You can modify your booking through your account dashboard or by contacting our customer support. Modifications may be subject to availability and additional charges.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer travel insurance options during the booking process. We recommend getting travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances.

How can I contact customer support?

Our customer support is available 24/7 through multiple channels: phone, email, and live chat. You can also visit our contact page for more information.
